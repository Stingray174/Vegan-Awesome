Why Be Vegan Awesome?

Meet Other Vegans

Meeting other likeminded people gives you an opportunity to share ideas and learn from each other. With us you can do all this in a fun and relaxed environment!

Over The Moon

Knowing you are doing something that's not only beneficial to you but also good for the environment, the animals and others including future generations will leave you feeling good and worthy. Purpose brings fulfillment!

An Apple A Day

A plant based diet is proven to be far less in cholesterol levels in comparison to animal products. Eating fruits and vegetables helps to keep you fit and strong ang more importantly feeling good from the inside out.

Get Active

Keep yourself fit and excited through the various activities that we do at our meetings and events. These range from beach cleaning, yoga, dancing and running amongst others.
